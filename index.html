By-Laws

1. Owners

There are ten owners: Andrew Abernathy, Carson Allen, John Carroll, John Egan, Jimmy Haverstick, Kent Mason, Todd Moran, Swan Nicolson, Steve Slayden, Steve Zamojski. Partners are welcome but there is one vote per team.

2. The Draft

Owners will draft 8 players to complete their team. A drawing will determine the order in which owner's choose their draft position. A team with neither owner or partner at the Draft will get the last available position. The draft order will reverse with each round.

3. The Season

The start of the season will be determined by the commissioner. The first half will conclude with The PGA Championship. The second half will conclude with The Tour Championship.

4. Scoring

4.1 For each tournament, players will score points based on the following:

  • 1st: 10 points
  • 2nd and ties: 5 points
  • 3rd and ties: 4 points
  • 4th and ties: 3 points
  • 5th and ties: 2 points

4.2 Any points earned by Scottie Scheffler will be multiplied by .5.

4.3 Points earned in the following tournaments will be multiplied by 1.5: The AT&T Pebble Beach Pro-Am, The Genesis Invitational, Arnold Palmer Invitational, RBC Heritage, Truist Championship, The Memorial Tournament, Travelers Championship.

4.4 Points earned in the following tournaments will be multiplied by 2: The Players Championship, The Tour Championship.

4.5 Points earned in the following tournaments "The Majors" will be multiplied by 3: The Masters, The PGA Championship, The US Open, The British Open.

4.6 Points will be accumulated by team to determine team position for first half, second half and overall.

5. Trades and Drops

5.1 Trades can be conducted up until the start of the British Open when a trade deadline will take effect. Trades are not subject to commissioner approval. The commissioner must be informed of a trade prior to the start of a tournament for the trade to be in effect during that tournament.

5.2 An owner can drop a player and pick up an unassigned player at any point during the season. There is a maximum of 5 drop/adds per season for each owner. Drop/Adds must be communicated by E-mail or WhatsApp to be official.

6. Money

6.1 Each owner will be responsible for $400/year for a total pot of $4000. The money will be distributed as follows:

  • 1st Half Winner: $550
  • 1st Half Runner-Up: $250
  • 2nd Half Winner: $550
  • 2nd Half Runner-Up: $250
  • Overall Winner: $1,300
  • Overall Runner-Up: $500
  • Major Winner: $150/Major

7. Commissioner Responsibilities

7.1 Whoever wins the league is the Commissioner for the next year. The Commissioner agrees to:

  • Set the date and plan the Draft trip.
  • Manage the Draft.
  • Keep score for the league and communicate scores at least every other week.
  • Provide final scores and payments within one week The Tour Championship.

8. The Last Night

On the Last Night of FG Draft Weekend, the team that finishes last will be responsible for providing a quality meal for all participants.

9. By-Laws

Any owner may propose a change to these by-laws and all owners will vote on the proposed change at the draft. Once the by-laws go into effect, any owner can change the by-laws by proposing a change via e-mail to all owners. A majority yes vote will change the by-laws.
